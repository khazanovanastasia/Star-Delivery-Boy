# Star Delivery Boy — Gameplay Mechanics

Star Delivery Boy is a rogue-like survival RPG focused on:
- surviving daily cycles
- balancing character needs
- exploring procedurally generated streets
- distributing “stars” without getting caught ??

## Daily Loop

1. Wake up in the apartment.
2. Prepare: clothing, items, food.
3. Leave home.
4. Explore the procedurally generated city.
5. Distribute stars.
6. Manage stats and consumables.
7. Return safely before time runs out.

## Core Systems

### Needs
- Health
- Hunger
- Craving 

### Stats
- Humility
- Agility
- Imagination
- Intuition
- Charisma

Stats influence:
- outcomes
- encounters
- prices
- movement
- perception

### Items
- Temporary buffs
- Clothing modifiers
- Consumables with side effects
